## 2. Moat Analysis (5 Types)

### 2.1 Brand/Pricing Power (Strongest)
- Search share: 91-93% globally (StatCounter 2025)
- Pricing: CPC CAGR ~5-8% over 5 years, advertisers have no bargaining power
- Brand: Google it = verb, BrandZ value ~$700B

### 2.2 Switching Costs (Extremely Strong)
- Android: ~3B devices, ~70% of global phones have Google pre-installed
- Chrome: ~3.3B users, default search engine nearly unshakable
- Gmail: ~1.8B users, deeply embedded in work/personal workflows
- Maps: ~1B+, local search + ads closed loop
- Apple takes ~$20B/year from Google for default search

### 2.3 Network Effects (Strong but not extreme)
- Two-sided: more advertisers -> more content creators -> more users
- Weaker than WeChat/Meituan; switching cost is habit, not social graph

### 2.4 Scale Effects (Extreme)
- ~35 data centers, own fiber network, TPU v6 reduces costs 30%+
- Marginal cost per search < $0.00001; per $1 revenue, <$0.05 cost
- Gemini training cost billions but negligible when spread across 3B users

### 2.5 Technology Moat (Strong but challenged)
- Search index: 1T+ pages (largest globally)
- TPU v6: one gen ahead; NVIDIA GPU ecosystem is more open
- Gemini 3.0: on par with GPT-5/Claude 4; OpenAI leads by ~6 months
- Waymo: #1 in autonomous driving (~30M miles); Cruise reviving, Baidu Apollo
- Risk signal: ChatGPT Search took ~2% share, growing fast

## 4. AI Impact on Search

AI Overviews: Search volume initially dropped ~15%, recovered to ~-5%.
CTR impact: ~7-10% decline initially. Google embedding ads in AI answers (May 2025).

3 scenarios:
- Bull: AI doubles search volume -> ad revenue doubles
- Base: CPC -30%, volume +50%, revenue +5%
- Bear: No clicks on links -> ad revenue -40%

Google internal estimate: Base to Bull case.

## 5. Second Growth Curve: Cloud + Waymo

Google Cloud: $43B revenue in 2025 (+25% YoY), first profitable year (~$2B OP).
Market share ~12% (3rd behind AWS ~32%, Azure ~23%). AI revenue contribution ~20%.
Duan: Heavy asset + long cycle biz, not Googles DNA. But AI differentiation (Gemini+TPU) is real.

Waymo: ~30M cumulative autonomous miles. Operating in SF/PHX/LA/Austin/Miami/DC.
~150K weekly paid rides. Cost/mile: $1.80 (down from $3.50 in 2023).
Cumulative losses > $50B. Duan: Option value, not core business. But if autonomous market hits $1T by 2030, Waymo is best positioned.
