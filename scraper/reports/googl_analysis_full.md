# Alphabet (GOOGL) Industry Analysis

> Perspective: Charlie Munger - moat and competitive barriers
> Data as of June 2026

---

## 1. Global Search Advertising Landscape

| Metric | Google | Bing | Yandex | DuckDuckGo | Others |
|--------|--------|------|--------|------------|--------|
| Global Share (StatCounter May 2025) | **89-90%** | 3-4% | 1% | <1% | <3% |
| Trend | First time below 90% in decade+ | Slight growth (Copilot) | Russia 73% market | Stable | - |
| Revenue Share | 86% of Alphabet revenue | <6% of MSFT | Minimal | Minimal | - |

**Key Insight:** Google's search moat comes from three barriers:
1. Data flywheel - 8.5B+ searches/day constantly improving ranking
2. Distribution monopoly - Apple Safari default ($20B+/year)
3. Advertiser lock-in - Google Ads/Maps ecosystem

**Risk:** AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity) opening cracks. AI answer cost is 10x+ traditional search. Business model not yet proven.

## 7. Industry Chain Position

| Layer | Google Role | Key Products | Moat Height | Revenue Potential |
|-------|------------|-------------|-------------|------------------|
| Infrastructure (Compute) | GCP + TPU | Cloud TPU v5p, TensorFlow | High (capital intensive) | Large but competitive |
| Platform/Model | Gemini + Vertex AI | Gemini series | Very High | Massive |
| Application | Search/YouTube/Workspace | Google Search, YouTube, Gmail | **Highest** | Largest (current) |

**Assessment by layer:**
- Infrastructure: 'Selling shovels' - good demand but margin compression over time
- Model layer: Core moat is data flywheel + app ecosystem integration, not just technology
- Application layer: Strongest moat - Google brand = search, YouTube creator lock-in

**Munger's core judgment:** Google is the only company occupying all three layers of AI (compute, model, application). This vertical integration gives the most options in any paradigm shift. Waymo is a potential bonus asset worth $100B+.

## 8. Key Conclusions

| Business | Competitive Edge | Risk | Score |
|----------|-----------------|------|-------|
| Search Ads | 90% monopoly + data flywheel | AI search paradigm shift | 4/5 |
| AI Search | AI Overviews + ecosystem | ChatGPT/Perplexity growth | 3/5 |
| Google Cloud | AI differentiation + growth lead | Share gap vs AWS/Azure | 3/5 |
| Gemini | Multimodal + long context + cost | GPT brand moat | 4/5 |
| YouTube | Dual format + creator ecosystem | TikTok competition | 5/5 |
| Waymo | Tech lead + commercial ops | Regulation + safety | 5/5 |

**Summary:** Alphabet is uniquely positioned across search monopoly, cloud growth, frontier AI models, video platform dominance, and autonomous driving leadership. The biggest risk is not competition - it's regulatory fragmentation and finding new business models in the AI era.
